=====createbool=====
Creates a boolean variable.\\

^Argument ^Description ^
|name|Name of the variable.|
|value|Initial value for the variable|
|help|Help shown when using help command on the console|

----
====Returns====
  * Handle to Variable\\

----
====Example====
<Code>
a=blackbox.createboolean('fullscreen', false, 'Controls fullscreen')
print(a)#would print 'False'
</Code>
