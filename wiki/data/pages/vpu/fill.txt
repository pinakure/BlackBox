=====fill=====
Fill selected bitmap with given color if specified or default color otherwise.\\ Specifying a color does not change selected color.

^  Argument  ^  Default  ^Description^
|  r  |  -1  |Red channel for fill color|
|  g  |  -1  |Green channel for fill color|
|  b  |  -1  |Blue channel for fill color|
|  a  |  -1  |Alpha channel for fill color|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0)
vpu.setcolor(64,0,0)
vpu.fill() #Fill with dark red
vpu.fill(255,255,0) #Fill with bright yellow
vpu.fill(-1,-1,-1,128) #Fill with dark red but half the alpha
</Code>
