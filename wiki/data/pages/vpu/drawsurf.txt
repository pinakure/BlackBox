=====drawsurf=====
Draw surface identified by given handle onto given coordinates

^Argument ^Description ^
|handle|Handle identifying the surface to be drawn|
|x     |Horizontal coordinate (in pixels) of selected surface |
|y     |Vertical coordinate (in pixels) of selected surface |

----
====Example====
<Code>
srf = vpu.createsurf(32, 32)
vpu.select(0)
vpu.drawsurf(srf, 32, 32)
</Code>
