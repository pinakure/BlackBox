=====line=====
Draw a line onto selected surface from x, y to dx, dy

^Argument ^Description ^
|  x  |origin horizontal coordinate|
|  y  |origin vertical coordinate|
|  dx  |end horizontal coordinate|
|  dy  |end vertical coordinate|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0)
vpu.setcolor(0,255,0)
vpu.line(0, 0, 100, 100)
vpu.update()
</Code>
