=====fillrect=====
Draw a filled rectangle onto selected surface from x,y to dx,dy using selected color as fill color.

^  Argument  ^Description^
|  x  |Horizontal origin, in pixels|
|  y  |Vertical origin, in pixels|
|  width  |Width of the rectangle to be drawn|
|  height  |Height of the rectangle to be drawn|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0)
vpu.setcolor(0,255,0)
w, h = vpu.dimensions()
vpu.fillrect(0,0,w,h) #Fill layer fully 
</Code>
