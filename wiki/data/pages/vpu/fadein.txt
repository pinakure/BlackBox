=====fadein=====
Fade screen from given color to fully transparent. If no color is specified, last color will be used.\\ 
Engine will not wait until fade ends, it is required to wait 2 seconds before changing screen contents if intended to be used to perform transitions.

^  Argument  ^  Default  ^Description^
|  r  |  -1  |Red channel for original color|
|  g  |  -1  |Green channel for original color|
|  b  |  -1  |Blue channel for original color|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.fadein(0, 0, 0) #Prepare fade settings
while(vpu.fading()):
	vpu.update() #Update fade and paint screen
</Code>
