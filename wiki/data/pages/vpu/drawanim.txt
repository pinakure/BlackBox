=====drawanim=====
Draw animation identified by given handle onto given coordinates

^Argument ^Description ^
|handle|Handle identifying the animation to be drawn|
|x     |Horizontal coordinate (in pixels) of selected surface |
|y     |Vertical coordinate (in pixels) of selected surface |

----
====Example====
<Code>
spr = vpu.createsprite('bottle')
ani = vpu.createanim(spr, 32, 32, 0, 0, 0, 7, False)
vpu.select(0)
vpu.drawanim(ani, 32, 32)
</Code>
