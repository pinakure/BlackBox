=====pset=====
Draw a pixel onto selected surface onto given coordinates

^Argument ^Description ^
|  x  |Horizontal coordinate in selected surface to draw onto |
|  y  |Vertical coordinate in selected surface to draw onto |

----
====Example====
<Code>
#Draws a diagonal line 16 pixel long
vpu.select(0)
for x in range(0, 16):
	vpu.pset(x, x)
vpu.update()
</Code>
