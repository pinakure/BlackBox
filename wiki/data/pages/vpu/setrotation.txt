=====setrotation=====
Sets rotation for specified layer or surface at given degrees.

^Argument ^Description ^
|  layer_or_handle  |0-2: Default layers, or surface handle|
|  angle  |Rotation in radians|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.setrotation(0, 3.141596) #Set rotation to 180 degree for layer 0
</Code>
