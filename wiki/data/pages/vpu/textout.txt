=====textout=====
Draw given text at given x, y coordinates in the selected video surface

^Argument ^Description ^
|  text  |Text to be typed on the selected surface |
|  x  |Horizontal coordinate |
|  y  |Vertical coordinate |

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0)
vpu.textout("hello", 16, 16)
vpu.update()
</Code>
