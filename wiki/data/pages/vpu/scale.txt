=====scale=====
Change specified layer or surface given horizontal and vertical scale factor

^Argument ^Description ^
|  layer_or_handle  |0-2: Default layers, or surface handle|
|  x  |Horizontal scaling factor (default = 1.0)|
|  y  |Vertical scaling factor (default = 1.0)|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.scale(0, 1.0, 1.0) #Set scale 1:1 to layer 0
</Code>
