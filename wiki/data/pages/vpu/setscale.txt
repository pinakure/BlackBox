=====setscale=====
Sets scale for specified layer or surface given horizontal and vertical values

^Argument ^Description ^
|  layer_or_handle  |0-2: Default layers, or surface handle|
|  x  |Horizontal scaling factor (default = 1.0)|
|  y  |Vertical scaling factor (default = 1.0)|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.setscale(0, 1.0, 1.0)
</Code>
