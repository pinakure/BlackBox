=====createsurf=====
Creates a video surface object.\\
A video surface object can perform blits((copying itself onto other surface)), therefore it is intended as target for any drawing routine.\\ 

^Argument ^Description                        ^
|width    |Desired surface width, in pixels   |
|height   |Desired surface height, in pixels  |

----
====Returns====
  * Handle to Initialized Surface 
  * Negative on error

----
====Example====
<Code>
srf = vpu.createsurf(32, 32) #Create 32x32 video surface
</Code>
