=====rect=====
Draw a rectangle onto selected surface from x,y to dx,dy using selected color as border color.

^  Argument  ^Description^
|  x  |Horizontal origin, in pixels|
|  y  |Vertical origin, in pixels|
|  width  |Width of the rectangle to be drawn|
|  height  |Height of the rectangle to be drawn|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0)
vpu.setcolor(0,255,0)
w, h = vpu.dimensions()
vpu.rect(0,0,0+w,0+h) #Draw a rectangle around the whole selected layer 
</Code>
