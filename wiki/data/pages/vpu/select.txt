=====select=====
Select given layer or surface to perform next graphic operations onto it

^Argument ^Description ^
|  layer_or_handle  |0-2: Default layers, or surface handle|

----
====Example====
<Code>
vpu.select(0) #Select background layer
vpu.select(1) #Select foreground layer
vpu.select(2) #Select overlay layer
srf = vpu.createsurf(128,128)
vpu.select(srf) #Select "srf" surface 
</Code>
