=====createsprite=====
Create Sprite object taking data from 'data/sprites/<filename>.png'\\ 
Full Explanation

^Argument^Description^
|filename|relative filename to be loaded as image data (png extension)|

----
====Returns====
  * Handle to Initialized Surface 
  * Negative on error

----
====Example====
<Code>
srf = vpu.createsurf(32, 32) #Create 32x32 video surface
</Code>
