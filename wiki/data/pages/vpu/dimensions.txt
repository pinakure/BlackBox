=====dimensions=====
Get currently selected target surface geometry values\\ 
To be called after selecting a surface. 

----
====Returns====
  * [width, height]  
  * Undefined if no surface is selected previously

----
====Example====
<Code>
#asuming an initialized VPU object at 640x480 resolution
vpu.select(0) #select default background layer
print(vpu.dimensions) # Would print: [640, 480]
</Code>
