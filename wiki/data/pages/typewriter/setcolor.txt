=====setcolor=====
Sets current typewriter color scheme

^Argument ^Description ^
^  Argument  ^  Default  ^Description^
|  r  |  -1  |Red channel for selected color|
|  g  |  -1  |Green channel for selected color|
|  b  |  -1  |Blue channel for selected color|
|  a  |  -1  |Alpha channel for selected color|
